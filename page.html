SPY on Calls

MobiSpy gives you an opportunity to chek and view all inbound and outbound calls, their duration and timestamp. This application can also be set up to record calls made to/from a number you have monitored. Record and see all call logs in the smartphone with phone number date and time.

Specail ability:

  • Call History
  • Record Calls
  • Intercept Live Calls
  • Block incoming Calls
